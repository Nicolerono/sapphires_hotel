Explore the water lavishly

Cruise along the mombasa coast aboard a luxurious yacht. Sip a cool refreshment and relax in one of the boat’s lounges while you take in the sights. The onboard chef will prepare lunch that’s accompanied by Caribbean music and refreshing hand-crafted cocktails.
