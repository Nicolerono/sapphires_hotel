GARDEN FACING ROOM

The garden facing rooms surround our quiet pool and our lush oasis of a spa. This area is ideal for guests wanting more calm Whilst these are the smallest of our rooms they are cosy with colorful touches, each with sliding glass doors opening up into your private balcony.

POOL FACING ROOM

The Pool Facing Rooms are located amongst colourful Ginger Lilies and frangipani trees overlooking the picturesque palms pool. The fresh colours and spacious rooms have an airy feel with high ceilings and glass doors that open up to your private balcony, overlooking the Pool, a myriad of trees and partially, the ocean.

STUDIO SUITE

Relax in your private haven with ocean views in one of our three airy and contemporary Studio Suites. The suites are located on the ground, 1st and 2nd floors, each with a double bed and a sofa made to transform into a bed. Baby cots can be added upon request.

PRESIDENTIAL SUITE

Our two presidential suites are set apart on a mini "Island" of their own complete with 2 luxurious master bedrooms, each ensuite with a jacuzzi and large terraces in a serene setting. The large lounge in each suite is attached to a kitchenette and bar which can be stocked with any specific beverages requested. Exclusive car parking slots are located nearby, and a private butler is assigned and available round the clock to ensure a most comfortable stay. These suites also have two back rooms with twin beds and rear exit doors..

ONE BEDROOM SUITE

TWe have three one bedroom suites close to the reception and sea facing on the ground floor. Each has a separate lounge and bedroom. The lounge has its own toilet. Both lounge and bedroom have a tv each.

SEA FACING ROOM

The Sea Facing Rooms have a light and fresh feel, each with a treasured private balcony. As the name would suggest these rooms have wonderful views of the expansive tropical waters of the Indian Ocean and you can sit back on your deckchairs and soak up the view at leisure.
