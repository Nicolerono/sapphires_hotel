Explore the ocean

A white sandy beach, palm trees, and colorful boats on a crystal sea – West Bay is an enchantment. But the show is at its finest underwater: multi-colored coral cascading in the deep blue water, parrotfish in abundance, shoals of tang and several species of angelfish, and all in exceptional visibility. West Bay is a must for snorkeling in Diani, and certainly one of the most beautiful snorkeling locations in the Mombasa. The Sapphires Hotel is just footsteps from this fantastic spot..
